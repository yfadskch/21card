21 Point Game

Game Rules:

1. Select a chip (50, 100, 200) to place your bet.

2. Hit to draw a card or Stand to hold your hand.

3. Win if your score is closer to 21 than the dealer's without going over.

4. You gain points equal to half of your bet.

5. Credit increases or decreases based on the result of the round.
